About This Project

Reimagining how agreements get signed

This project focused on designing an electronic signature platform that handles document preparation, signing workflows, and secure storage for legally binding agreements.

Electronic signing has become essential for modern business, but many solutions still feel clunky. Our goal was to create an experience so smooth that signing feels effortless while maintaining the security and compliance businesses require.

The Objective

What we set out to achieve

Design an e-signature experience so intuitive that users can send, sign, and manage documents without reading a single instruction.

The platform needed to handle complex multi-party signing workflows while remaining simple enough for first-time users. Integration with existing document workflows and compliance with e-signature regulations were essential.

07
Ideation & Problem Solving

From insights to solutions

The ideation phase focused on eliminating friction at every step—from document upload to final signature. We prioritized mobile-first design given the growing trend of signing on phones.

Guided Signing

Step-by-step flow that highlights each field requiring action, eliminating confusion about what to do next.

Smart Field Detection

AI-powered field suggestions that auto-detect where signatures, dates, and initials should go.

One-Tap Templates

Pre-configured templates for common documents that let users send for signature in under 30 seconds.

10
Reflections

What this project taught me

The best signature experience is one you barely notice—sign and done.

This project reinforced that simplicity requires significant design effort. Every eliminated step and auto-detected field represents hours of design iteration and technical collaboration.

The key insight was that mobile isn't just a smaller screen—it's a different context. People sign documents while commuting, in meetings, or between tasks. Designing for these moments changed our entire approach.
