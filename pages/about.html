About Me

I'm Prateek Singh, a product designer with 6+ years of experience crafting intuitive digital experiences that solve real-world problems. As Principal Product Designer at Compliance Innovation, I blend strategic thinking with user-centered design to transform complex challenges into seamless, beautiful solutions.

My Journey

My passion for design began with a simple question: "How can we make technology more human?" Starting as a Software Developer and UI Designer, I quickly realized that creating beautiful interfaces wasn't enough I needed to solve problems and delight users.

Over six years, I've evolved from designing static screens to leading end-to-end product experiences. I've worn many hats: UX designer, front-end developer, design systems architect, and strategy partner. This diverse background has given me a unique perspective on how design, development, and business objectives align to create meaningful impact.
