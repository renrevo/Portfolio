Background

I led the design for a multi-asset Trading Platform enabling users to trade Private Stocks, SBA7 Loans, and Crypto assets. The product served a wide range of stakeholders including retail investors, brokers, super admins, and financial institutions.

I collaborated closely with real traders, brokers, and product managers to design trading flows that are not only compliant and secure but also intuitive and streamlined for both experienced and first-time users.

Problem & Objectives

Designing an intuitive and secure experience for complex asset classes such as private stocks, loans, and crypto. Ensuring compliance, simplifying workflows, and reducing friction for both novice and advanced investors were top priorities.

Design Process

Research & Discovery

Conducted multiple meetings with private equity brokers, SBA loan traders, and crypto stakeholders to understand industry workflows and user needs.

User Flows & Wireframes

Mapped trade lifecycle for each asset type and iteratively tested wireframes to balance usability and compliance requirements.

UI Design

Created scalable component libraries and responsive UIs for mobile and web. Introduced modular design to support expansion to new asset classes.

Collaboration

Worked closely with developers to handle edge cases like partial wallet custody in crypto transactions and SPV participation in Private stocks trades.

Results & Impact

  • Reduced trading setup time by 40% for brokers via SPV simplification
  • Improved investor confidence through transparent UI for crypto transactions
  • Successfully launched and used by real-world traders across three asset types

Reflection

This project strengthened my ability to design in high-complexity environments. It also taught me how to communicate and validate designs with both domain experts and engineers to deliver real, functioning products at scale.
