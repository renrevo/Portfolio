About This Project

Streamlining Identity & Compliance for Global Scale

This project focused on designing a unified compliance platform handling KYC (Know Your Customer), KYB (Know Your Business), AML (Anti-Money Laundering), Investor accreditation, PEPs (Politically Exposed Persons) & Sanctions verification processes across 200+ countries, onboarding trusted users in under 30 seconds.

Regulatory compliance has traditionally been a friction point in financial services. Our mission was to create an experience that guides users through complex verification workflows while maintaining the highest security and compliance standards.

10
Reflections

What this project taught me

Good compliance UX is invisible; users should feel guided, not interrogated.

This project taught me that the best compliance design is neutrality combined with power. By treating identity as a dynamic, extensible layer rather than a static step, we enabled businesses to adapt to global regulations instantly.

The key insight was that users aren't frustrated by compliance itself; they're frustrated by the lack of clarity and transparency in the process. Clear expectations and visible progress transformed an obligation into a manageable task.
