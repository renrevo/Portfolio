About This Project

Transforming regulatory compliance into seamless experiences

This project focused on designing a unified compliance platform handling KYC (Know Your Customer), KYB (Know Your Business), AML (Anti-Money Laundering), Investor accreditation, PEPs (Politically Exposed Persons) & Sanctions verification processes.

Regulatory compliance has traditionally been a friction point in financial services. Our mission was to create an experience that guides users through complex verification workflows while maintaining the highest security and compliance standards.

Ideation & Problem Solving

From insights to solutions

The ideation phase focused on breaking down complex verification into manageable steps while providing users with clear guidance and instant feedback at every stage.

Progressive Verification

Step-by-step flows that break complex verification into achievable milestones with clear progress tracking.

Smart Document Capture

Real-time document validation with instant feedback on image quality, document type, and completeness.

Contextual Guidance

In-context help and examples that reduce confusion and prevent common submission errors.

10
Reflections

What this project taught me

Good compliance UX is invisible; users should feel guided, not interrogated.

This project taught me that regulatory complexity doesn't have to translate to user complexity. By investing heavily in contextual guidance and error prevention, we created a verification experience users actually praised.

The key insight was that users aren't frustrated by compliance itself; they're frustrated by the lack of clarity and transparency in the process. Clear expectations and visible progress transformed an obligation into a manageable task.
